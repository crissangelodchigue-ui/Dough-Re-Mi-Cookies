About Dough-Re-Mi Cookies 🍪

Discover the story, passion, and people behind every freshly baked cookie.

Our Story

Dough-Re-Mi Cookies began with a simple love for baking and sharing happiness. Inspired by family recipes and unforgettable childhood memories, we started creating cookies that bring comfort, joy, and connection.

What began as homemade treats for friends and family quickly grew into a passion-driven bakery dedicated to creating premium handcrafted cookies.

Today, Dough-Re-Mi Cookies continues to spread smiles one cookie at a time.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To create handcrafted cookies using premium ingredients while delivering happiness, comfort, and unforgettable experiences to every customer.

Vision

To become the most loved local cookie brand known for creativity, quality, innovation, and exceptional customer experiences.
